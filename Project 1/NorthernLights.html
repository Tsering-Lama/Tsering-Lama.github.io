The Northern Lights

Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year round. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location. Add even more adventure to your stay in ICeland and come explore more...

The Otherworldly aurora borealis, or the northern lights, begin high in the Earth's atmosphere- at altitudes from 60 to more than 250 miles- when charged particles from the sun become trapped in the Earth's magnetic field. The result is a colorful dancing light show.
