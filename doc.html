Login and Registration Guide

Welcome to Baruch College's Course Portal! Below are the instructions for logging in and registering.

Logging In:

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to the homepage of the course portal.
  2. Locate the "Log In" button at the top-right corner of the page and click on it.
  3. Enter your username and password in the designated fields.
  4. Click on the "Login" button to access your account.

Registering:

  1. From the homepage, click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" link.
  2. Provide basic information such as your name, email address, desired username, and password.
  3. Review the information and accept the terms and conditions.
  4. Click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" button to create your account.
  5. Verify your email address by clicking on the verification link sent to your email inbox.

Troubleshooting:

  1. If you encounter any issues, look for help options such as "Forgot Password" links or "Contact Us" sections.
  2. Ensure that you're entering the correct credentials and that your internet connection is stable.

Additional Tips:

  1. Use a strong and unique password to secure your account.
  2. Keep your login credentials confidential and avoid sharing them with anyone.
  3. Regularly update your password for added security.
  4. Familiarize yourself with the features and navigation of the course portal.

If you have any further questions or encounter any difficulties, feel free to reach out to the support team for assistance.

Happy learning!
